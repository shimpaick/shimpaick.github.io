Our immigration practice covers the full range of matters affecting individuals, families, and employers in the New York metropolitan area and beyond.

Family-Based Immigration

We assist U.S. citizens and lawful permanent residents in sponsoring family members for immigrant visas and green cards.

  • Immediate relative petitions (I-130)
  • Adjustment of status
  • Consular processing
  • Conditional residence removal (I-751)

Employment-Based Visas

We represent employers and foreign nationals in obtaining work authorization and permanent residence through employment.

  • H-1B specialty occupation
  • L-1 intracompany transfers
  • O-1 extraordinary ability
  • EB-1, EB-2, EB-3 preference categories

Naturalization & Citizenship

Guiding eligible lawful permanent residents through the citizenship process from application through the oath ceremony.

  • N-400 application preparation
  • Interview preparation
  • USCIS representation
  • Citizenship documentation

Removal Defense

Representation before the immigration courts and the Board of Immigration Appeals for individuals in removal proceedings.

  • Cancellation of removal
  • Asylum and withholding
  • Appeals to the BIA
  • Federal court review
