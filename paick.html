Ieback grew up in the Bronx and attended Columbia University, followed by New York University School of Law. He was admitted to the New York bar in 2001 and has dedicated his career to immigration law, representing individuals, families, and employers in a wide range of immigration matters.

Ieback focuses his practice on family-based immigration, employment visa petitions, naturalization, and removal defense. He is known for his methodical approach and for maintaining close, direct communication with every client throughout the life of a case.

He is a member of the American Immigration Lawyers Association, the New York State Bar Association, and the New York City Bar Association's Immigration Committee.
